Product Features
  • Generate hashtags serving general purpose

    Assists users to use a set of hashtags by processing their images.

  • Text categorization

    Classifies text into five main categories : sports, business, education, entertainment and technology.

  • Generate hashtags serving commercial purpose

    Solution for social media business users to generate trending and relevant hashtags.

Benefits

  • The proposed system uses image processing to generate hashtags rather than requesting keywords from the user. Therefore, what separates the proposed system from most of the existing systems is the fact that the user does not need to ponder over any input except the image.



  • The proposed system helps fetch trending hashtags to extend the commercial reach of upcoming potential online sellers. Instagram is used extensively for such product based promotional activities. The concept of hashtags began from twitter and continues to evolve in this space. Therefore, tags are commercialized to produce lucrative results by bringing in a variety of customers.

  • The hashtag itself is like a filter on Twitter. Searching a specific hashtag on twitter produces an output set containing information from all fields of work in relation to the tag searched. The model classifies this information into different categories based on their nature. Therefore, it becomes convenient for the user to restrict their feed intake.
